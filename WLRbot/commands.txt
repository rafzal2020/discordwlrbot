These are the following commands you can use:

**!wlr** - Tells you how many days have passed since Whole Lotta Red was announced.

**!add (song link) or !as** - Add a song to the list of songs

**!queue or !q** - Tells you all the songs in the list.

**!clear or !c** - Clears the list of songs.

**!howmany or !hm** - Tells you the AMOUNT of songs in the list

**SONG OF THE DAY IS AUTOMATICALLY SENT EVERY DAY AT 1 A.M.**
